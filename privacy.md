# Tempo — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 19, 2026**

Tempo ("the App") is provided by **Aslanov Ventures LLC** ("we," "us," "our," or "the Company"), operated by Farid Aslanov. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Tempo is an **adults-only (18+)** personal daily-planning assistant. By using the App you confirm that you are at least 18 years old.

If you have any questions, contact us at **faridaslanov@icloud.com**.

---

## 1. Our privacy principles

- **On-device first.** Your planning data lives on your device. We do not operate a general-purpose server that stores your schedule, chats, or logs.
- **No tracking.** We do not track you across apps or websites. The App contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, no IDFA/AdSupport, and no App Tracking Transparency prompt because we do not track.
- **No ads. No data sale.** We do not show ads and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.
- **Minimal sharing.** Data leaves your device only to deliver the features you ask for — chiefly, generating AI plans and replies.

---

## 2. Information the App handles

**a. Account information (Sign in with Apple).**
Signing in is optional. If you choose to sign in, Apple provides us with your **name** and **email address** and a stable account identifier. We use these to identify your account and, when signed in, to authenticate your AI requests. You can use much of the App without signing in.

**b. Your planning content (stored on your device).**
The schedule blocks you create, chat messages, reflections, reminders, "wins"/progress, spending entries, and health logs (such as weight) you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This content stays on your device except as described in Section 3.

**c. Context sent to AI features.**
When you use an AI feature (for example, asking Tempo to plan your day or replying in chat), the App sends the **text of your request and relevant context** — your chat message, the relevant part of your schedule, and, where relevant to your request, **health or spending context** (for example, a recent weight or a budget figure) — to our AI service so it can generate a useful response. See Section 3 for how this is routed.

**d. What we never collect or send to AI.**
**Progress photos are never sent to AI features and never leave your device for AI processing.** We do not collect your contacts, precise location, browsing history, or device advertising identifiers.

---

## 3. How AI features work (and who processes your data)

Tempo's AI features are powered by **Anthropic's Claude API**. To protect your privacy and keep credentials off your device, AI requests are routed through **a secure proxy operated by the Company on Cloudflare Workers**. The proxy forwards your request to Anthropic and returns the response. It exists to hold the API credential and route the call — it is not a database of your life.

- **Anthropic** is our **sole third-party AI processor.** It processes the request you send in order to generate a response. Under Anthropic's commercial API terms, your inputs and outputs are not used to train its models.
- **Apple** provides Sign in with Apple. Apple's handling of that sign-in is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.
- **Cloudflare** provides the network infrastructure for the proxy.

We do not send your data to any other third party, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

---

## 4. How we use information

We use the information above only to:

- Provide the App's core features (building and adjusting your schedule, chat, reminders, reflections, spending and health tracking).
- Generate AI plans and replies you request.
- Authenticate your account when you sign in.
- Respond to your support requests and content reports.
- Keep the App secure and working correctly.

We do **not** use your information for advertising, cross-app tracking, or sale.

---

## 5. Data retention and deletion

- **On your device:** Your planning content remains until you delete it or uninstall the App. The App includes **"Delete my data,"** which permanently erases your schedule, chats, reflections, wins, spending, health logs, reminders, and profile from the device.
- **Account data:** If you signed in and choose to delete your account, the App also requests deletion of the account data associated with your sign-in from our proxy.
- **AI requests:** Request content is processed to generate a response and is subject to Anthropic's and Cloudflare's operational retention as data processors; we do not maintain our own store of your AI conversations.

## 6. Your choices and rights

- **Use without signing in** for most features.
- **Access and edit** your data directly in the App at any time.
- **Delete** your data in-app ("Delete my data"), and delete your account if you signed in.
- **Report content** you find objectionable via the in-app "Report a problem" option or by long-pressing a message, or by emailing us.
- **Decline AI features** by simply not using them — your other data stays on-device.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, deletion, or portability). To exercise them, email **faridaslanov@icloud.com**.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including storing credentials in the device Keychain, sending all network traffic over encrypted connections (HTTPS), and keeping the AI API credential on the server-side proxy rather than on your device. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.
